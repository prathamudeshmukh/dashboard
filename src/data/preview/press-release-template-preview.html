InnovaTech Solutions Raises $50M Series B to Accelerate Global Expansion
Funding will support product development and entry into five new international markets
SAN FRANCISCO, CA — April 1, 2024

InnovaTech Solutions, a leading provider of enterprise automation software, today announced the closing of a $50 million Series B funding round led by Summit Capital Partners, with participation from existing investors Horizon Ventures and Catalyst Fund.

The company plans to use the proceeds to accelerate product development, expand its go-to-market team, and establish offices in London, Singapore, Toronto, Sydney, and Berlin over the next 18 months.

"This investment validates our vision to make enterprise automation accessible to businesses of every size. We're building the infrastructure that will power the next decade of digital transformation."
— Michael Torres, CEO of InnovaTech Solutions

InnovaTech's platform currently serves over 1,200 enterprise customers across 30 countries, processing more than 50 million automated workflows per month. Year-over-year revenue grew 185% in 2023.

About InnovaTech Solutions:

InnovaTech Solutions provides enterprise-grade automation software that helps organisations streamline operations, reduce manual effort, and accelerate growth. Founded in 2019 and headquartered in San Francisco, the company employs 340 people globally.

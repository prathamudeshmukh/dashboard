Nexus Technologies Raises $50M Series B to Expand AI-Powered Workforce Platform

Funding will accelerate product development and international expansion into Europe and Asia

SAN FRANCISCO, CA — April 25, 2025 —

Nexus Technologies, the leading AI-powered workforce management platform, today announced the close of a $50 million Series B funding round led by Sequoia Capital, with participation from Andreessen Horowitz and existing investors.

The company will use the funding to accelerate its product roadmap, expand its engineering team by 80 engineers, and launch operations in the United Kingdom and Japan by Q3 2025.

Nexus currently serves over 1,200 enterprise customers across 35 countries, processing more than 10 million workforce decisions per month.

This investment validates our vision that AI-powered workforce management is the future. We are just getting started.

— Sarah Kim, CEO & Co-founder, Nexus Technologies

The Series B brings Nexus's total funding to $78 million. The company is on track to achieve profitability by Q4 2025.

Earlier this year, Nexus was named to the Forbes AI 50 list and the Fast Company Most Innovative Companies list.

About Nexus Technologies

Nexus Technologies (nexustech.ai) is the AI-powered workforce management platform trusted by 1,200+ enterprises. Founded in 2020, Nexus has processed over 50 million workforce decisions and saves customers an average of 15 hours per week per manager.
